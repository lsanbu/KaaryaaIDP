**Current Code Logic (main.py):**
1.  **Identity Response Model:** Extracts `id_number`, `full_name`, `dob`, `address`, `ifsc`, `micr`, `warnings`.
2.  **Routing:** Auto-classifies document types (Aadhaar vs. PAN vs. Cheque).
3.  **Aadhaar Strategy:** Uses "Anchor Logic" (Finds 'DOB' line, looks at the line above for Name) to fix OCR fragmentation. Includes "Consent Evidence" extraction.
4.  **PAN Strategy:** Regex validation for PAN pattern.
5.  **Cheque Strategy:** "Hunter-Seeker" Regex to find IFSC/Account Numbers in unstructured layouts (CTS-2010).
6.  **SRE Features:** Includes `validation_status`, `confidence_score`, and fallback logic (Human-in-the-loop triggers).
